Register

Step #1

Connect to clover.gg and use the command /register . Make sure you're using an email that you have access to.

If you use an incorrect email while signing up, you must wait 2 minutes before using the command again. If you're banned, you can still join the server through the ban-hub.

Step #2

Login on to your email and look for a email from admin@clover.gg. Click on the link provided in the email and continue your signup process.

Check your spam folder if you cannot find the email, or wait 2 minutes to use the command again.

Step #3

Entire a valid password and then finish your signup process. After, you will be presented with a login screen, just re-type your details and your logged in!

Ensure that the password is at least 3 characters long, and is a good password. Your password is safe with us!
